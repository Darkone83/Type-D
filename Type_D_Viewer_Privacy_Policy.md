# Type D Viewer – Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2025-11-21_

## Overview
Type D Viewer respects your privacy. This application does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. All data displayed within the app remains on your device at all times.

## Information We Do Not Collect
### No Personal Data
Type D Viewer does not collect or request:
- Your name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Account information
- Contact lists
- Photos, camera data, or files

### No Tracking
We do not:
- Track you across apps or websites
- Use analytics services
- Use advertising identifiers
- Collect usage statistics
- Implement third-party tracking SDKs

### No Location Access
Type D Viewer does not access your GPS or location services.

## Local Network Access
Type D Viewer uses **Local Network** permissions strictly for the app’s core functionality:  
**discovering and reading telemetry from compatible devices on your local network.**

The app does **not**:
- Transmit this data outside your device  
- Store it remotely  
- Share it with third parties  

All telemetry data is processed **entirely on your device** and is discarded when the app is closed.

## Data Storage
Type D Viewer:
- Does **not** upload data  
- Does **not** store any logs  
- Does **not** save data outside the app’s runtime  
- Does **not** use cloud storage or external servers  

Sensitive values displayed within the app (such as identification keys) are shown **locally only** and may be masked by default for user safety.

## Third-Party Access
No data is shared with:
- External servers  
- Advertisers  
- Analytics providers  
- Third-party partners  

We do not sell or disclose any information because **we do not collect any information** in the first place.

## Children’s Privacy
Type D Viewer does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. The app is safe for all users.

## Changes to This Policy
If the privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be included with an updated release of the app. Type D Viewer will never weaken your privacy protections without informing you.
